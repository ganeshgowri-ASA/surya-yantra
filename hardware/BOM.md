## Ordering notes

1. **ESL-Solar 500** has a 10–14 week lead time from ET SolarPower
   (Shenzhen). Raise the PO at T-16 weeks.
2. **Omron G9EA-1-B** — order from element14 India or Mouser, not local
   distributors (counterfeits seen in Nehru Place market).
3. **MC4 connectors** — only genuine Stäubli / Multi-Contact parts; the
   generic "MC4-style" Chinese imports are not UL listed.
4. **Pyranometers** — book calibration certificate (2-year ISO 17025)
   with the order; add ~₹ 18 k per unit.
5. **Rack cooling** — India summer requires ≥ 4000 W cooling capacity. If
   room AC is below 1.5 ton, add a dedicated in-rack AC (LCP).
